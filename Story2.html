The Story of Zeus

Zeus, the king of the gods in Greek mythology, was the ruler of Mount Olympus and the god of the sky, lightning, thunder, law, and order. He was one of the principal gods of the Greek pantheon and fathered many famous heroes and gods.

Key Facts about Zeus:

  • Ruler of Mount Olympus
  • God of the sky, lightning, thunder, law, and order
  • Symbolized by the thunderbolt
  • Known for his numerous love affairs and offspring
  • Defeated his father Cronus and the Titans

Key Events in the Life of Zeus:

Event Description
Birth Born as the son of Cronus and Rhea
Defeat of Cronus Overthrew his father Cronus and the Titans
Defeat of Typhon Defeated the monstrous Typhon and restored order to the cosmos
