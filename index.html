Greek Mythology Overview

  1. Greek mythology is the foundation of Western literature, art, and culture.
  2. The story of Hercules is often referenced and adapted in Western art.
  3. Many constellations in the night sky are named after characters and creatures from Greek mythology.
  • Zeus is the king of the gods and the god of the sky, thunder, lightning, and justice.
  • Hera is the queen of the gods and the goddess of marriage, women, childbirth, and family.
  • The underworld is ruled by Hades, the god of the dead.
